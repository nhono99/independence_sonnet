About

PlayBunk is a decentralized game distribution platform that empowers video game communities and promotes agame developers an publishers. We are bringing together PC games and blockchain games in one platform.

With PlayBunk, gamers can enjoy a wide access to the content they want to play without having to worry about intermediary costs. We are focused on making games accessible to the community. We are also dedicated to equipping the next generation of developers and players with the tools they require to collaborate with fellow game creators and publish their games. We will also be one of the first platforms to accommodate the growing worldwide interest in blockchain game technology.

Watch us. We'll redifine your gamespace.
