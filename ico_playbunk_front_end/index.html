About

PlayBunk is a decentralized digital game distribution platform that aims to empower video game communities and promote game developers and publishers. We are bringing together PC games and blockchain games in one platform.

With PlayBunk, gamers can enjoy a wide access to the content they want to play without having to worry about intermediary costs. We are focused on making games accessible to the community. We are dedicated to equipping the next generation of developers and players with the channels they require to collaborate with fellow game creators and publish their games. We will also be one of the first platforms to accommodate the growing worldwide interest in blockchain game technology.

Imagine fun. Experience creativity.

FAQs

Q: What is PlayBunk?

A: PlayBunk is a decentralized digital platform for distributing and promoting PC games and blockchain games. It provides a highly competent avenue for gamers to acquire a wider variety of games, and for developers to receive higher margins as they showcase their products in our game store.

Q: How do I create an account?

A: No need to create an account. All you need is to install MetaMask, create your digital wallet and you're good to go.

Additional info: Keep your digital wallet created on MetaMask safe and secured. Make sure you don’t forget your password or the mnemonic words provided to you.

Q: How do I use BUNK Tokens in relation to your project?

A: Our PlayBunk ecosystem fully utilizes BUNK tokens in a variety of ways. Using BUNK tokens, users can buy PC games, utilize Creator's Lounge by collaborating and creating games, buy in-game items in our user-driven marketplace, buy and trade items in all of our blockchain games. Our tournaments will also reward players with BUNK tokens.

Q: How to acquire BUNK Tokens?

A: You can acquire BUNK Tokens by clicking the 'Buy Tokens' button and through our buy page. We accept Ethereum (ETH) and Bitcoin (BTC) payments.

The minimum contribution to acquire BUNK tokens is 0.10 ETH.

We are also in talks with several Cryptocurrency Exchanges and BUNK Tokens will soon be available in these sites.

Q: Are there any restricted countries in buying BUNK Tokens?

A: The following countries are restricted in participating in our token sale:

United States of America, People's Republic of China (except for Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan), South Korea, Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Crimea Region, Philippines.

Q: Soft cap and hard cap of BUNK Tokens

A: The soft cap is $3,000,000 while the hard cap is $30,000,000.

Q: How will the funds be used?

A: The funds will be used for development, marketing, business development, and strategic partnerships with 3rd party developers and publishers.

Q: When will you be on exchanges? Which exchanges will I be able to find you guys in?

A: We're currently in discussion with several exchanges. We are targeting to be available in major exchanges within 3 months after our token sale.

Q: How can I convert my BUNK Tokens into other cryptocurrencies?

A: PlayBunk will be available in several major exchanges. With these exchange platforms, users can easily convert Bunk tokens to traditional currencies or other digital currencies like Bitcoin(BTC), LiteCoin(LTC), etc.

Q: Is there a referral system? What are the ways of earning additional BUNK Tokens?

A: Yes, we have a Bounty Program for participants who are eager to gain additional BUNK tokens. For more information, kindly check Bounty Program.

Q: Can you explain your transaction fee in Creator's Lounge?

A: Unlike other freelancing platforms, there are no transaction or commission fees from our end in using Creator's Lounge.

Q: Is your token a security?

A: Definitely not. BUNK token is a 100% utility token used for transactions within PlayBunk platform ecosystem. Token holders receive no shares in the company, neither they are eligible to receive any kind of dividends or interest. There is no buyback program and we do not influence the price of the token in any way or form.
